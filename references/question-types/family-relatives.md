# Family and Relatives Module

## Scope

- Category ID: family-relatives
- Chinese label: 父母、亲属、家庭关系、家务矛盾。
- Use when the main question concerns a family member, relative, or household relationship.

## Trigger Questions

- 我妈现在对这件事是什么态度？
- 家里这次矛盾会不会缓和？
- 亲戚借钱这事能不能解决？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Romantic relationship belongs to `romance-marriage`.
- Money or debt primary belongs to `money-debt`.
- Health primary belongs to `health-medicine`.

## Question Subject

Fix the named relative before applying six-relative labels. A literal six-relative
name must not silently replace the real named person.

## Perspective

State whether the asker asks about self, parent, child, sibling, spouse, relative,
or household as a whole.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use the named person's actual role when directly asked. Use Shi/Ying for self
versus external relative when role-specific line is ambiguous. Use matter
relative for money, documents, health, or property.

## Secondary References

Six relatives can describe household roles, generation, peer conflict, resources,
authority, children, or documents after the named person is fixed.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If a named mother conflicts with 父母 line condition, report anchor ambiguity and
do not move to a different relative solely for a better story.

## Month and Day

Judge the named relative anchor and household matter line against month/day.
Weak or void family line caps certainty.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show active family communication, conflict, help, or change. It
does not guarantee reconciliation.

## Shi and Ying

Shi is asker's side. Ying may be external relative, opponent side, or family
condition outside the asker's control.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Combination/clash can show binding or conflict only when attached to the family
anchor. Hidden/void can show unspoken or delayed family matter.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Trigram family images can corroborate elder/younger or household tone but cannot
replace named relative anchors.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: relationship tendency, conflict type, communication caution, or matter
split. Exact private thoughts or guaranteed reconciliation are not allowed.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report real statements, boundaries, active conflict, weak anchors, money/legal
facts, or health facts.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not treat the six-relative name as literal identity when the question already
names a real person. Do not force family harmony from combination alone.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “我妈怎么看”：named mother first; attitude module may be secondary.
- “亲戚借钱”：relative anchor plus money module for debt.
- “家里矛盾”：household sides may require Shi/Ying and outcome module.
