# Health and Medicine Module

## Scope

- Category ID: health-medicine
- Chinese label: 健康、症状、检查、治疗、药物、医疗建议边界。
- Use when the main question concerns health, illness, symptoms, treatment, or medical results.

## Trigger Questions

- 我最近身体状态需要注意什么？
- 这个症状发展会不会加重？
- 家人这次检查结果要关注什么？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Family relationship itself belongs to `family-relatives`.
- Child or pregnancy context may add `children-pregnancy`.
- Legal/insurance disputes belong to `disputes-litigation` or `contracts-documents`.

## Question Subject

Separate symptom trend, medical test result, treatment decision, medication,
urgent safety issue, and emotional concern.

## Perspective

State who is the patient and whether the asker is self, family, or third party.
Medical facts and qualified clinicians govern.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 官鬼 for illness, pressure, danger, or pathological burden. Use 子孙 for
medicine, relief, recovery support, or removal of distress when appropriate.
Use 父母 for test reports and medical documents.

## Secondary References

Body/trigram images may describe symbolic area only. Month/day and movement
judge trend, obstruction, or relief.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If symptom line and medicine/report line conflict, state both and defer real
medical decisions to clinical evidence.

## Month and Day

Judge illness and relief anchors against month/day; strong illness or weak
relief warrants caution, not diagnosis.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement may show symptom change, treatment activity, or report development.
It does not prove a medical diagnosis.

## Shi and Ying

Shi may show the patient if self; Ying may show doctor, external condition, or
named outside patient depending on perspective.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void/hidden/tomb may show unclear, latent, delayed, or hard-to-detect issue, but
not a definitive disease.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Body correspondences from Shuo Gua are symbolic imagery only. They cannot
diagnose organs, prescribe medicine, or replace tests.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: symbolic caution, trend class, need to verify, or seek medical care.
Do not give diagnosis, prescription, or instruction to delay urgent care.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report real symptoms, medical test results, clinician advice, emergency signs,
medicine use, and uncertainty.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not diagnose, prescribe, stop medication, delay urgent care, or claim medical
certainty from a hexagram.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “症状会不会加重”：官鬼 trend and 子孙 relief, with explicit medical disclaimer.
- “检查结果”：父母 report line plus timing if waiting for results.
- “家人健康”：patient perspective and family module may be secondary.
