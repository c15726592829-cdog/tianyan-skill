# Study and Exams Module

## Scope

- Category ID: study-exams
- Chinese label: 学业、考试、录取、证书、成绩、复习。
- Use when the main question concerns learning, exams, admission, or academic/certificate results.

## Trigger Questions

- 这次考试能不能通过？
- 我复习方向对不对？
- 录取通知大概什么时候有消息？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Job interview belongs to `work-career`.
- Administrative certificate approval belongs to `contracts-documents`.
- Strategy between study plans may add `strategy-comparison`.

## Question Subject

Separate preparation, examination performance, admission, certificate, official
result, and study method.

## Perspective

State whether the asker is examinee, applicant, parent, teacher, or third party.
Official notices and score rules govern real outcomes.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 父母 for study materials, certificates, exam papers, admission notices, and
formal results. Use 官鬼 for pressure, rules, examiner/authority, or competitive
threshold. Use 子孙 for relief, performance comfort, or release from pressure
only when appropriate.

## Secondary References

Shi shows the student's capacity and effort. Ying may show school, examiner, or
external condition. 兄弟 may show competitors.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If preparation is good but official document/authority is weak, separate
learning state from result approval.

## Month and Day

Judge 父母/官鬼/世爻 strength against month/day to assess readiness and official
result possibility.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show studying, review changes, exam process, or result issuance.
It does not guarantee score or admission.

## Shi and Ying

Shi is student/applicant side. Ying can be school, exam board, admission office,
or external selection.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void or hidden 父母 can indicate delayed notice, missing material, unclear result,
or weak documentary support.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may describe mental clarity, pressure, delay, or method fit, but do not
replace actual study effort and official criteria.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: pass tendency, preparation weakness, likely notification window, or
study focus. Do not guarantee scores, admission, or certification.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report weak student capacity, damaged 父母, strong competition, real score gap,
deadline, or missing application material.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not advise ignoring study plans, exam rules, registration deadlines, or
official notices.
