# Strategy and Comparison Module

## Scope

- Category ID: strategy-comparison
- Chinese label: 策略、选择、方案比较、行动建议。
- Use when the user asks what to do, which option to choose, or how to act.

## Trigger Questions

- 我现在应该主动联系还是等待？
- 选A方案还是B方案更稳？
- 这件事我是继续推进还是先查合同？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- If the user only asks what will happen, route to the matter module or
  `outcomes-development`.
- Do not cast separately for each option when one structured comparison answers
  the question.
- High-stakes decisions require professional and factual checks.

## Question Subject

List all options exactly as stated. Define what “better”, “稳”, “合适”, or
“应该” means: success, risk, speed, cost, safety, or relationship impact.

## Perspective

State the asker's controllable action, external constraints, and reversibility
of each option.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use the matter-specific anchor for the domain, then compare options by how they
affect Shi, Ying, useful spirit, risk line, timing, and real constraints.

## Secondary References

Movement of Shi can show user's action; Ying shows external response. Documents,
money, health, legal, or relationship modules may be secondary.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If one option has higher upside and higher risk, say so directly. Do not collapse
strategy into a single lucky/unlucky label.

## Month and Day

Judge whether the selected action has current support or whether waiting for an
activation window is more defensible.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Use moving lines to identify active pressure, available action, or backlash.
Do not recommend action solely because a line moves.

## Shi and Ying

Shi movement supports action by the asker; Ying movement supports waiting for
external action. Generation/control shows support or resistance.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void/break/tomb may support waiting, verification, or preparation. Clash may
support opening or disruption depending on strength and target.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may inform tone: advance, stop, clarify, communicate, verify, reduce
exposure, or seek help. They are not commands.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: compare options, recommend proportionate reversible action, define
what to verify, and state when not to act. Do not coerce, gamble, or ignore facts.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report high-stakes risk, missing documents, medical/legal/financial facts,
explicit boundaries, weak action line, and irreversible consequences.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not tell the user to take unsafe, illegal, financially reckless, coercive, or
medically risky action based on a chart.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “主动联系还是等”：compare Shi-action evidence with Ying-action evidence and real boundaries.
- “A还是B”：both options must be predeclared; compare under same evidence hierarchy.
- “继续推进还是查合同”：contracts-documents may be secondary and can override symbolic optimism.
