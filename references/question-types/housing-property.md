# Housing and Property Module

## Scope

- Category ID: housing-property
- Chinese label: 房屋、租赁、买卖、房产、产权、居住状态。
- Use when the main question concerns housing condition, renting, buying, selling, or property procedures.

## Trigger Questions

- 这套房子适不适合买？
- 租房这件事能不能谈成？
- 房产证手续什么时候能办好？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Contract or certificate procedure may add `contracts-documents`.
- Payment and affordability may add `money-debt`.
- Legal disputes may add `disputes-litigation`.

## Question Subject

Separate physical house condition, lease negotiation, purchase/sale, title
record, documents, price, payment, defects, and moving-in timing.

## Perspective

State whether the asker is buyer, seller, tenant, landlord, agent, or family
member. Real inspection, title records, and contracts govern.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 父母 for house, property documents, title certificates, vehicles, and formal
records. Use 妻财 for price/payment, 官鬼 for authority/risk, and Ying for
counterparty.

## Secondary References

Shi/Ying relation shows negotiation. Money, document, authority, and defect
indicators must be separated.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If house/document line is strong but money/counterparty is weak, answer property
quality and deal completion separately.

## Month and Day

Judge 父母, money, and counterparty anchors against month/day for stability,
procedural progress, and risk.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Moving 父母 can show house/document change. Moving money or counterparty lines
can show negotiation, payment, or deal movement.

## Shi and Ying

Shi is asker's side; Ying is seller, landlord, buyer, tenant, agency, or external
property condition.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Hidden or void 父母 may show unseen defect, missing document, delayed title, or
unclear property status.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Trigram and natural images can describe house environment or defect imagery only
as secondary support.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: suitable/not suitable tendency, risk focus, required verification,
negotiation caution, or timing window. Do not replace inspections or legal checks.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report title uncertainty, inspection issues, contract terms, price risk, weak
父母, strong 官鬼, or real deadlines.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not advise buying, selling, renting, or ignoring defects solely from a
favorable chart.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “房子适合买”：父母 house line plus money, documents, and inspection facts.
- “房产证什么时候”：contracts-documents and timing are secondary or primary depending on wording.
- “租房谈成”：Ying counterparty plus 父母 house and outcome evidence.
