# Money and Debt Module

## Scope

- Category ID: money-debt
- Chinese label: 钱财、收款、债务、收益、破财、资金。
- Use when the main question concerns money, resources, debt recovery, payment, or financial loss.

## Trigger Questions

- 这笔钱还能收回来吗？
- 我最近会不会破财？
- 合作方什么时候把款项打过来？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Business relationship itself belongs to `business-partnership`.
- Contract terms and documents belong to `contracts-documents`.
- Investment, legal, or major financial decisions need explicit high-stakes limits.

## Question Subject

Distinguish proceeds, salary, debt recovery, affordability, expense, loss, cash
flow, and partner payment. State the money holder and expected transfer.

## Perspective

Shi is the asker's side. Ying may be debtor, payer, client, partner, or outside
counterparty.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 妻财 for money, goods, revenue, proceeds, or financial resources. Use Ying or
counterparty line for payer/debtor behavior.

## Secondary References

父母 may represent contracts, invoices, receipts, or proof. 兄弟 may represent
competition, division, or money drain. 官鬼 may represent risk, pressure, or
legal/official obstruction.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If money line is strong but payer/action line is weak, separate “money exists”
from “money is received”.

## Month and Day

Money line strength, payer line strength, and activation by month/day govern
recoverability and timing.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show payment process, loss, blockage, transfer, or competition.
Moving 兄弟 can indicate division or drain when it damages 妻财.

## Shi and Ying

Shi receiving generation from money or payer is different from Ying holding or
blocking money. State direction of flow.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void, tomb, break, or hidden money line can show unavailable funds, delay,
concealment, or uncertain recoverability.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may describe scarcity, flow, blockage, or resource condition but cannot
override actual balances and contracts.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: recoverable/unrecoverable tendency, partial/full, delay, risk type, and
documents to verify. Do not give investment guarantees or precise gains.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report missing contract, debtor refusal, weak 妻财, strong 兄弟/官鬼 obstruction,
or real financial risk.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not advise risky financial action based on a favorable symbol. Do not treat
symbolic money as actual available cash.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “钱能不能回来”：妻财 is money; Ying/payer and 父母 proof decide collection path.
- “什么时候打款”：add `timing`; timing from activation of payer or money line.
- “合作收益”：may require `business-partnership` plus money line.
