# Disputes and Litigation Module

## Scope

- Category ID: disputes-litigation
- Chinese label: 纠纷、投诉、官司、仲裁、证据、调解。
- Use when the main question concerns conflict, dispute resolution, complaint, litigation, or procedure.

## Trigger Questions

- 这个纠纷最后能不能解决？
- 对方会不会继续投诉我？
- 这次官司证据准备够不够？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Contract documents may add `contracts-documents`.
- Strategy choices may add `strategy-comparison`.
- Workplace disputes may add `work-career` when employment is primary.

## Question Subject

Separate parties, claim, evidence, procedure, authority, deadline, settlement,
cost, and outcome.

## Perspective

State the asker's legal or dispute role: claimant, respondent, complainant,
defendant, mediator, employee, customer, or family member.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 官鬼 for legal pressure, authority, official process, accusation, danger, or
opposing pressure. Use 父母 for evidence, documents, contracts, filings, and
notices. Use Ying for opposing party.

## Secondary References

Shi/Ying relation shows parties. Money, document, authority, and action lines
must be separated for settlement and procedure.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If evidence is strong but authority/procedure is weak, distinguish factual
support from procedural outcome.

## Month and Day

Judge authority, evidence, opponent, and asker's capacity against month/day.
Weak or void evidence line caps confidence.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show complaint escalation, procedural action, evidence submission,
or settlement movement. It does not prove legal outcome.

## Shi and Ying

Shi is asker's side. Ying is opponent, complainant, counterparty, or external
condition. Control between them indicates pressure or conflict.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Combination may bind parties; clash may open, scatter, or intensify. Judge by
strength and relevance. Hidden evidence can be unavailable or undisclosed.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may describe conflict tone but cannot replace law, deadlines, evidence,
or professional advice.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: risk level, procedural caution, evidence gap, settlement tendency, or
need for professional advice. Do not give legal conclusions.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report legal deadlines, official notices, missing evidence, weak 父母, strong
官鬼, and real attorney/court information.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not tell the user to ignore legal deadlines, filings, summonses, contracts,
or qualified legal advice because of a chart.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “官司证据够不够”：父母 evidence line and 官鬼 authority line both matter.
- “对方投诉”：Ying actor plus contact/action and dispute pressure.
- “纠纷能不能解决”：outcome module may be secondary, legal constraints govern.
