# Business and Partnership Module

## Scope

- Category ID: business-partnership
- Chinese label: 合作、合伙、项目、客户、伙伴。
- Use when the main question concerns partnership, counterpart reliability, business project, or cooperation.

## Trigger Questions

- 这个合作项目能不能成？
- 和这个伙伴继续合伙靠谱吗？
- 对方会不会按合同推进合作？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Payment or profit alone belongs to `money-debt`.
- Contract wording, signing, or paperwork belongs to `contracts-documents`.
- Pure success/failure of a non-business matter belongs to `outcomes-development`.

## Question Subject

Separate counterpart, project, resources, proceeds, contract, execution, risk,
competition, and final outcome.

## Perspective

Shi is the asker's side or company. Ying is counterpart, client, partner, or
external project side.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use Ying for partner/counterpart. Use 妻财 for profit/resources, 父母 for
documents/contracts, 官鬼 for authority/risk, and 兄弟 for competitors or resource
division as required by the concrete question.

## Secondary References

Movement, generation/control between Shi and Ying, contract/money lines, and
competition lines determine cooperation quality.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

Do not reduce partnership to one line. If partner is favorable but money or
contract is weak, answer each layer separately.

## Month and Day

Judge counterpart, project matter, money, and document anchors against month/day
before deciding feasibility.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Relevant movement can show negotiation, execution, conflict, payment, contract
progress, or withdrawal.

## Shi and Ying

Shi-Ying relation is central: generation can support cooperation; control can
show pressure; clash can show instability; combination can bind or entangle.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Combination/clash must be judged with strength and relevance; hidden or void
partner/project evidence caps reliability.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images can describe project atmosphere, movement, blockage, or resource quality
only as secondary evidence.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: cooperate/avoid/verify, risk focus, likely obstacle, or conditions for
success. Do not guarantee profit or legal enforceability.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report weak counterpart, damaged contract, weak money line, strong competitors,
real due diligence gaps, and contract uncertainty.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not conclude “合作必成” from Shi/Ying combination alone. Do not ignore signed
terms, invoices, or legal obligations.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “合伙靠谱吗”：Ying partner plus money/contract/risk lines.
- “项目能不能成”：if primary is business, use project/counterpart layers before outcome.
- “按合同推进”：requires `contracts-documents` and `contact-actions` as secondary dimensions.
