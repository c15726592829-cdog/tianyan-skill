# Romance and Marriage Module

## Scope

- Category ID: romance-marriage
- Chinese label: 感情、恋爱、复合、婚姻、关系发展。
- Use when the main question concerns romantic relationship state or development.

## Trigger Questions

- 我和他感情后面会怎么发展？
- 这个人是不是适合继续谈？
- 我们会不会复合并联系上？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- A pure reply/contact question belongs to `contact-actions`.
- A pure attitude question belongs to `thoughts-intentions`.
- A pure person-identity question belongs to `people-identity`.

## Question Subject

Separate attraction, present relationship, contact, commitment, marriage, and
final outcome. These are not the same answer.

## Perspective

Lock the asker's side, counterpart, current real-world relationship, and asked
time window before choosing the useful spirit.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use Shi/Ying for the two sides of the relationship. Use six relatives only when
the question specifically asks role matters such as resources, pressure,
documents, children, or family.

## Secondary References

Combination/clash, generation/control, motion, and relevant relationship matter
lines can corroborate attraction, conflict, delay, or separation.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If traditional spouse-relative imagery conflicts with Shi/Ying and the real
relationship context, Shi/Ying and stated context govern.

## Month and Day

Judge whether Shi, Ying, and any selected relationship matter line are supported
or damaged by month/day.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show emotional activity or situational change. It does not prove
contact, reconciliation, commitment, or marriage without action/outcome support.

## Shi and Ying

Read Shi-Ying relation first: generation, control, combination, clash, distance,
and movement from either side. Then check matter-specific evidence.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void, break, tomb, hidden spirit, or blocked combination can cap certainty and
separate lingering feeling from manifest relationship.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Use hexagram and trigram images as emotional or situational color only after
the structural relationship judgment.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: current tendency, obstacle type, likely development class, practical
strategy. Exact commitments, marriage dates, or partner identity require
separate evidence and are usually capped.

## Required Contrary Evidence

State explicit rejection, no-contact reality, existing partner, distance,
weak/void Ying, strong opposing line, or unsafe/coercive context.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not infer sex or relationship identity from 妻财 or 官鬼 alone. Do not treat
symbolic attachment as consent, promise, or actual relationship recovery.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “会不会复合”：judge Shi/Ying and relationship outcome; if also asking contact, add `contact-actions`.
- “他喜不喜欢我”：primary may be `romance-marriage` or `thoughts-intentions`; state which resolution is being answered.
- “是不是男朋友”：identity is not proven by 官鬼; require predeclared candidates or real facts.
