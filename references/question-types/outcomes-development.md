# Outcomes and Development Module

## Scope

- Category ID: outcomes-development
- Chinese label: 成败、结果、发展、转机、走势。
- Use only when the primary question is genuinely success/failure or overall development.

## Trigger Questions

- 这件事最后能不能成？
- 后面还有没有转机？
- 我换方案后结果会不会更好？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Do not use this as a fallback for unresolved classification.
- If the matter is work, money, relationship, contract, health, travel, or study,
  route primary to the specific module and add this only as secondary.
- Strategy comparison belongs to `strategy-comparison`.

## Question Subject

Define what “成”, “结果”, “转机”, or “更好” means before interpreting. Separate
opening, progress, manifestation, and secured final result.

## Perspective

State who benefits, what event counts as success, and what time window applies.
If success criteria are vague, classification confidence is low.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use the matter-specific useful spirit selected from the actual event. If the
matter is unknown, ask to narrow; do not invent a generic outcome line.

## Secondary References

Month/day, motion, Shi/Ying, original/taboo spirits, branch structures, and real
facts decide whether the selected matter can manifest.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If several outcomes are embedded in one question, split them or cap confidence.
Do not mix the most favorable sign from each outcome.

## Month and Day

Judge whether the selected matter has strength, support, activation, or damage.
Month/day are primary before narrative optimism.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Relevant movement can show development, obstruction, reversal, or progress.
Movement of unrelated lines does not decide final result.

## Shi and Ying

Shi/Ying relation can show self versus external condition, but the matter anchor
still governs the outcome.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void/break/tomb/hidden conditions may delay or block manifestation. Combination
and clash require context and cannot alone prove success.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Hexagram/trigram images describe overall tone only after structural judgment.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: likely/unlikely/mixed, opening versus secured result, obstacle type,
condition for improvement, and practical next step.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report vague success criteria, weak matter anchor, no activation, strong taboo
spirit, real-world constraints, or missing facts.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not use outcomes-development as a universal fallback. Do not equate “有转机”
with “最终成功”.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “这件事能不能成”：first identify what “事” is; then choose the proper matter anchor.
- “有没有转机”：separate activity/opening from final secured result.
- “换方案会不会更好”：strategy module may be primary because options are being compared.
