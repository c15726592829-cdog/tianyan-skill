# Work and Career Module

## Scope

- Category ID: work-career
- Chinese label: 工作、职业、面试、岗位、领导、职场发展。
- Use when the main question concerns employment, role, authority, workplace, or career outcome.

## Trigger Questions

- 我这份工作后面会稳定吗？
- 这次面试有没有希望？
- 领导对我这个方案什么态度？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Salary, proceeds, debt, or payment belongs to `money-debt`.
- Contracts, offers, certificates, and paperwork belong to `contracts-documents`.
- Leader's attitude may add `thoughts-intentions`.

## Question Subject

Separate current job, job search, interview, promotion, leader, workload,
colleague, authority, document, and money. They use different anchors.

## Perspective

State whether the asker is employee, applicant, contractor, manager, or business
counterpart. Preserve the current employment facts.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 官鬼 for position, authority, pressure, employer power, or official role.
Use 父母 for certificates, offers, paperwork, and policies. Use 妻财 for salary
or proceeds only when money is the object.

## Secondary References

Shi/Ying shows self versus organization/counterpart. 兄弟 may show colleagues or
competition. Movement and month/day judge whether the opportunity manifests.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If the question mixes leader attitude and job outcome, answer attitude and job
result separately or lower confidence.

## Month and Day

Judge the job/authority anchor against month/day before reading workplace
images. Weak official anchor caps promotion or offer confidence.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement of 官鬼, 父母, Shi, Ying, or relevant competition lines can show
interview process, pressure, offer, or job change.

## Shi and Ying

Shi is the asker's capacity and position. Ying is employer, interviewer,
external organization, or condition outside the asker's control.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void or hidden 官鬼/父母 can show unclear authority, delayed offer, hidden policy,
or unavailable position.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may describe workplace atmosphere, movement, blockage, or hierarchy, but
do not override job-anchor strength.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: favorable/mixed/unfavorable tendency, obstacle type, preparation focus,
or bounded timing if timing module supports it. Do not guarantee employment.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report weak job anchor, strong competition, document obstruction, real interview
facts, deadlines, or missing qualifications.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not infer a guaranteed offer or promotion from 官鬼 appearing alone. Do not
use divination to ignore labor contracts, HR notices, or professional advice.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “面试有没有希望”：官鬼/父母 and Shi/Ying decide opportunity and procedure.
- “领导态度”：counterpart attitude may require `thoughts-intentions`.
- “工资会不会发”：primary should be `money-debt`, not work-career.
