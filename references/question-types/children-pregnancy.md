# Children and Pregnancy Module

## Scope

- Category ID: children-pregnancy
- Chinese label: 子女、孩子、备孕、怀孕、养育事项。
- Use when the main question concerns children, pregnancy, parenting, or child-related outcomes.

## Trigger Questions

- 孩子这次入园能不能顺利？
- 我们备孕这件事需要注意什么？
- 孩子的身体状态要不要尽快复查？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Child health or pregnancy medical care must add `health-medicine`.
- Child exam/school performance may add `study-exams`.
- Family relationship alone belongs to `family-relatives`.

## Question Subject

Separate child welfare, school, pregnancy, fertility, parenting decision,
medical risk, and family support.

## Perspective

State whether the asker is parent, prospective parent, relative, or third party.
Pregnancy and child health are high-stakes.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 子孙 for children, juniors, relief, and child-related matters. Use 父母 for
school documents, admission, or certificates. Use 官鬼/子孙 carefully for illness
and relief in health contexts.

## Secondary References

Shi shows parent's capacity or asker's side. Ying may show school, doctor,
external institution, or counterpart.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If child matter and medical matter conflict, medical safety and clinical facts
govern. Do not use symbolic signs to avoid care.

## Month and Day

Judge 子孙, medical/report, and institution anchors against month/day for trend
and support; avoid certainty.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Movement can show child matter activity, school process, family action, or
medical process. It is not proof of pregnancy, fetal status, or diagnosis.

## Shi and Ying

Shi may represent parent/asker. Ying may represent institution, doctor, or
external condition.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void/hidden/tomb can show uncertainty or delayed manifestation; it cannot rule
pregnancy or child health in or out.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Trigram family or body images are symbolic only and cannot determine fetal sex,
diagnosis, or medical urgency.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: symbolic caution, process tendency, practical verification steps, and
need for professional care. Do not predict fetal sex or medical outcomes.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report medical symptoms, pregnancy tests, pediatric signs, school notices,
deadlines, and clinician advice.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not predict fetal sex, diagnose pregnancy or illness, delay care, or replace
pediatric/obstetric advice.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “孩子入园”：子孙 plus 父母 document/school evidence.
- “备孕注意什么”：high-stakes; give cultural reading plus medical verification.
- “孩子身体”：primary or secondary `health-medicine`; professional care governs.
