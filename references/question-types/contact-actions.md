# Contact and Actions Module

## Scope

- Category ID: contact-actions
- Chinese label: 联系、回复、来找、执行、行动。
- Use when the main question is whether an actor will do something observable.

## Trigger Questions

- 他今天会不会联系我？
- 对方还会来找我吗？
- 我主动发消息后他会不会回复？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Attitude without observable action belongs to `thoughts-intentions`.
- Relationship outcome belongs to `romance-marriage`.
- Exact timing belongs to `timing` when it is the primary request.

## Question Subject

Fix actor, action, recipient, and time window. If actor or action is ambiguous,
classification confidence is low.

## Perspective

Shi is the asker's side. Ying or a named-person anchor is the outside actor.
For the asker's own action, Shi may be the actor.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use Ying for counterpart action, Shi for asker's own action, and a matter line
for action toward a concrete thing such as document, money, travel, or work.

## Secondary References

Movement, changed line, day/month activation, and Shi/Ying relation are required
to move from symbolic connection to possible action.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If Ying shows attitude but no effective movement, answer “connection/attention”
separately from “will act”.

## Month and Day

The actor anchor must be capable of manifesting: supported, activated, or
released from void/break. Weak or damaged actor evidence caps action confidence.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Effective action normally needs relevant movement or activation. Movement by an
irrelevant line is not evidence that the named actor will act.

## Shi and Ying

Ying moving toward Shi can support external approach; Shi moving toward Ying can
support the asker's initiative. Generation/control describes ease or friction.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void or tombed actor/action evidence may show delay or non-manifestation unless
there is a clear filling, clash, or activation rule.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Use images such as movement, speech, obstruction, or communication only as
secondary support after actor and motion evidence.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: likely/possible/unlikely action within stated window, likely channel,
or condition needed. Exact minute/date requires `timing` evidence and is capped.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report no movement, weak actor, void/break, real-world blocking, explicit refusal,
or lack of feasible channel.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not conclude contact from symbolic liking, combination, or Ying presence
alone. Do not turn a thought reading into an action prediction.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “他会不会联系我”：Ying is actor; require Ying movement or activation toward Shi/contact channel.
- “我主动发消息他回不回”：Shi initiates; Ying's capacity and response relation decide reply, not the asker's wish.
- “对方会不会签”：actor may be Ying, document may be 父母, outcome may require `contracts-documents`.
