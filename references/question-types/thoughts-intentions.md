# Thoughts and Intentions Module

## Scope

- Category ID: thoughts-intentions
- Chinese label: 想法、态度、意图、心理倾向。
- Use when the requested answer is attitude or intention rather than external action.

## Trigger Questions

- 对方现在对这件事是什么态度？
- 他心里到底怎么想我？
- 刘硕士对这次出行是什么想法？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Actual contact, reply, departure, or execution belongs to `contact-actions`.
- Relationship development belongs to `romance-marriage`.
- Timing belongs to `timing` as primary only when “什么时候” is the main question.

## Question Subject

Fix whose attitude is being asked. For a named outside person, use Ying as the
person anchor, then read action/matter evidence only as secondary.

## Perspective

State whether the asker wants the other person's attitude toward self, toward a
matter, or toward a third party. Do not claim direct mind-reading.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use Ying for the counterpart or outside subject. Use the matter-specific useful
spirit when the attitude is toward a concrete matter such as work, money,
travel, or contract.

## Secondary References

Movement, generation/control, combination/clash, and six spirits can describe
observable orientation: interest, resistance, hesitation, pressure, concealment,
or readiness.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If the counterpart anchor and matter anchor disagree, answer attitude and matter
feasibility separately; do not force one line to explain both.

## Month and Day

Strong supported Ying can show active external stance. Weak, void, broken, or
tombed Ying caps certainty about current attitude.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Ying moving, relevant matter line moving, or direct relation to Shi may show
active attention. Movement does not prove contact or actual behavior.

## Shi and Ying

Shi-Ying generation, control, combination, or clash can describe relational
orientation, but only with strength and context.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Void or hidden attitude evidence may mean unexpressed, delayed, concealed, or
unclear stance; do not equate it with absence of thought.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Trigram images and six spirits may color the attitude but cannot override
Ying/matter anchor strength.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: supportive/resistant, open/closed, hesitant/active, likely intention
class. Not allowed: verbatim private thoughts or guaranteed future action.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report weak Ying, unmoved counterpart, strong obstruction, real-world explicit
statements, or lack of action evidence.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not say “他一定在想你”, “她肯定会行动”, or quote private inner speech from symbols.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “对方怎么看我”：Ying is the counterpart; relation to Shi shows orientation; action requires contact-action evidence.
- “朋友对出行什么想法”：Ying anchors friend; travel line is secondary.
- “领导对方案态度”：Ying or 官鬼/父母 may be secondary depending on whether the question targets leader or document/plan.
