# Contracts and Documents Module

## Scope

- Category ID: contracts-documents
- Chinese label: 合同、文书、证件、审批、手续、材料。
- Use when the main question concerns documents, signing, procedures, approvals, or formal records.

## Trigger Questions

- 这份合同能不能顺利签？
- 我的证件审批什么时候下来？
- 房屋买卖手续会不会卡住？

## Exclusions and Redirects

- Money transfer belongs to `money-debt`.
- Housing substance belongs to `housing-property`.
- Business counterpart reliability belongs to `business-partnership`.

## Question Subject

Separate document existence, signing, approval, authority, deadline, money,
counterparty action, and final result.

## Perspective

State whether the asker is applicant, signer, counterparty, employee, buyer,
seller, or agent.

## Primary Anchor or Useful Spirit

Use 父母 for documents, certificates, contracts, forms, procedures, houses, and
formal records. Use 官鬼 for authority/official pressure where relevant. Use
妻财 only for proceeds/payment.

## Secondary References

Ying may show counterparty. 官鬼 may show regulator, employer, court, or authority.
Timing triggers show approval or signing windows.

## Anchor Conflict Resolution

If document line is strong but authority or counterparty line is weak, answer
“paperwork exists/is prepared” separately from “approval/signing completes”.

## Month and Day

父母 and authority lines must be supported or activated to show smooth procedure.
Void/break indicates delay, missing material, or blocked approval.

## Moving and Changed Lines

Moving 父母 can show document progress or change. Moving 官鬼 can show official
pressure or decision. Movement alone does not guarantee approval.

## Shi and Ying

Shi shows asker's preparation/capacity. Ying shows counterparty or external
approval side when applicable.

## Branch Structures, Void, Break, Tomb, and Hidden Spirit

Hidden or void 父母 can show missing document, unseen clause, delayed certificate,
or incomplete procedure.

## Hexagram and Trigram Corroboration

Images may describe paperwork complexity, obstruction, clarity, or delay, but
formal records and deadlines govern real decisions.

## Allowed Answer Resolution

Allowed: smooth/delayed/blocked, missing material, authority issue, likely window
if timing supports it. Do not override legal or administrative deadlines.

## Required Contrary Evidence

Report weak/void 父母, authority obstruction, missing documents, unsigned terms,
deadlines, or real procedural requirements.

## Prohibited Inferences

Do not tell the user to ignore signed terms, filing deadlines, legal notices, or
official instructions because of a favorable chart.

## Worked Structural Examples

- “合同能不能签”：父母 for contract, Ying for counterparty, 官鬼 for authority/risk.
- “证件什么时候下来”：父母 plus timing activation.
- “房屋手续”：primary may be contracts-documents; housing module covers property substance.
