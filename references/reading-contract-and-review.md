# Reading Contract and Outcome Review

This protocol applies to every Tianyan interpretation. It improves
reproducibility without adding another divination method or claiming scientific
predictive validity.

## Question Lock

Before selecting a category, person or matter anchor, or useful spirit, record:

- the exact user question;
- the question subject and perspective;
- the concrete matter or event;
- the requested time window;
- the requested resolution and maximum defensible resolution;
- known real-world facts;
- whether an outcome, answer, candidate, or ground truth has already been revealed.

If ambiguity would change the primary category, anchor, useful spirit, time
window, or scoring resolution, ask one focused question before interpretation.
Otherwise state the smallest reasonable assumption and proceed.

The lock occurs before chart interpretation. After locking, do not silently
rewrite the question, change perspective, switch anchors, narrow or widen the
time window, or raise the answer resolution to obtain a more favorable fit.

In Accuracy Test Mode, the existing pre-reveal case contract remains the
governing scoring record. This question lock supplies its interpretation inputs.

## External Context Boundary

External information is admissible only when recorded before the conclusion and
labeled as a known fact, real-world constraint, or contrary evidence. It is not
an additional symbolic or divination layer.

External context must not:

- replace Najia, Shi/Ying, useful-spirit, month/day, movement, changed-line, or
  branch-structure analysis;
- import Meihua external-omen rules or justify `舍卦从应`;
- change the locked category, perspective, anchor, or useful spirit merely to
  fit a preferred answer;
- justify a new cast or repeated cast; external context does not justify a recast;
- be added after outcome disclosure and represented as pre-conclusion evidence.

If a verified real-world fact contradicts symbolic inference, preserve both in
the record, lower confidence, and let reality govern practical advice. A
symbolic conclusion cannot override medical evidence, legal obligations,
documents, explicit behavior, financial controls, or safety facts.

## Ordinary Outcome Review

Use this review when feedback arrives outside explicit Accuracy Test Mode. It is
diagnostic and does not create a predictive-accuracy score.

Record:

```text
Original exact question:
Original cast time and timezone:
Original locked category, perspective, and anchor:
Original conclusion, resolution, confidence, and time window:
Outcome reported verbatim:
Match at the original resolution:
Evidence that held:
Evidence that failed:
Failure classes:
Retrospective hypothesis:
Prospective test needed:
```

Always preserve the original conclusion. Do not replace it, switch anchors
after feedback, reinterpret a broad class as an exact hit, or count a
post-outcome explanation as a correct pre-outcome judgment.

Use one or more existing failure classes from the accuracy-testing protocol,
including question or perspective error, anchor error, useful-spirit error,
calendar error, movement/static misuse, branch overreach, unsupported timing,
resolution overreach, ignored real-world contradiction, or genuine ambiguity.

A lesson from one review remains a retrospective hypothesis. It may be promoted
to a governing rule only after evaluation on a future unrevealed case under the
accuracy-testing protocol and an explicit source or rule update. Apply the same
proposed rule to future successes and failures.

## Method Boundary

This protocol does not authorize Meihua Ti-Yong, mutual hexagrams, numerical
trigram casting, trigram-number timing, external-omen casting, or any mixed
method that replaces Wenwang Liuyao Najia.
