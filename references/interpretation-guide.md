## Perspective And Evidence Labels

Preserve the user's exact question. Identify the asker, named subject, external
counterpart, matter, requested resolution, and time window before selecting a
useful spirit. For a named friend or other outside person, normally use Ying as
the person-anchor; use a matter-specific relative as secondary evidence.

Label each material statement as chart fact, classical rule, inference,
contrary evidence, or speculation. State the anchor before interpretation and
do not change it after ground truth is revealed.

## Select The Useful Spirit

Choose the useful spirit from the concrete object of the question, not from the
result the user wants.

Common traditional correspondences:

| Matter | Primary reference |
|---|---|
| self, own position, own action | 世爻 |
| parents, elders, documents, contracts, certificates, houses, vehicles | 父母 |
| children, juniors, relief, medicine, enjoyment, removing adversity | 子孙 |
| office, authority, career position, pressure, illness, danger | 官鬼 |
| money, goods, revenue, resources, traditionally wife | 妻财 |
| siblings, peers, partners, competitors, division of resources | 兄弟 |
| other party or external counterpart | 应爻 plus matter-specific relative |

Modern relationship questions are context-sensitive. Do not select 妻财 or 官鬼
solely from gender. Use the user's role, the relationship being asked about,
Shi/Ying, and the matter-specific relative; state the choice.

Six relatives express roles relative to the palace element. They do not prove a
person's sex or exact relationship identity.

If the question combines several distinct outcomes, narrow it before judging or
give separate, weaker conclusions.

## Four Supporting Roles

After selecting the useful spirit:

- 原神: generates the useful spirit
- 忌神: controls the useful spirit
- 仇神: supports the taboo spirit or damages the original spirit
- 闲神: has no direct governing role in the current question

These roles depend on the selected useful spirit and can change with a different
question. Do not permanently label a line favorable or unfavorable.

## Establish Strength

Judge in this order:

1. month commander
2. day branch
3. movement and changed branch
4. generation and control from relevant lines
5. combination, clash, void, break, tomb, and hidden status

Ask:

- Is the useful spirit seasonally supported?
- Does the day support, control, combine, clash, or fill it?
- Is it moving or static?
- Does it receive effective generation?
- Is it controlled by an active, strong taboo spirit?
- Is it empty, month-broken, tombed, hidden, or unable to manifest?

Do not count favorable and unfavorable signs mechanically. Authority, activity,
and direct relevance matter more than the number of symbols.

## Read Movement

For every moving line that touches the main matter:

1. identify its visible relative and branch
2. identify what it changes into
3. decide whether the change returns generation, return control, advances,
   retreats, repeats, empties, breaks, combines, or clashes
4. decide whether it acts on the useful spirit, Shi, Ying, original spirit, or
   taboo spirit

Movement indicates activity, not automatic success. A moving taboo spirit can
intensify obstruction; a moving useful spirit can still become controlled or
retreat.

Static does not mean absent. Separate representation, presence, activation,
and ability to manifest.

## Read Shi And Ying

- Shi represents the questioner's position, capacity, or immediate side.
- Ying represents the counterpart, target, or external conditions.
- Generation suggests support or flow; control suggests pressure or conflict.
- Combination suggests attachment or binding; clash suggests separation,
  activation, or instability depending on strength.
- Shi moving emphasizes the user's action.
- Ying moving emphasizes external or counterpart movement.

Do not conclude that a person will act merely because Ying has symbolic
connection. Require movement, strength, and real-world feasibility.

## Question Types

### Result

Answer whether the useful spirit can manifest and what blocks or enables it.
Separate "there is an opening" from "the result is secured."

### State

Describe the present balance only. Do not force a long-term prediction from a
question about current status.

### Strategy

Translate the chart into bounded actions:

- advance, wait, reduce exposure, verify, communicate once, prepare documents,
  seek professional advice, or stop

Do not recommend repeated testing, coercion, financial risk, or unsafe action.

### Relationship

Combine Shi/Ying with the selected relationship relative. Keep these
distinctions explicit:

```text
有感应不等于有行动
有想法不等于会联系
有余气不等于关系恢复
相合不等于现实承诺
```

### Work, Contract, And Money

Use 官鬼 for position/authority, 父母 for documents/contracts, 妻财 for proceeds
or money, and 兄弟 for competition or resource division as the question
requires. Real documents, deadlines, and financial controls take priority.

### Health And Safety

Traditional correspondences may be discussed, but never diagnose, prescribe,
or delay urgent care. Direct the user to qualified help and observable facts.

## Timing

Give tendencies and windows, not guaranteed dates.

Potential timing cues include:

- useful spirit becoming seasonally supported
- leaving void or being filled/clashed
- month-broken branches becoming restored
- moving lines meeting their branch, combination, or clash
- changed branches becoming active

State why a window is selected. If several timing rules conflict, lower the
confidence rather than choosing the most attractive date.

Do not infer an exact date from a matching branch alone. Require the selected
reference, an activation rule, a calendar unit, and no stronger contradiction.

## Confidence

Use:

- **强**: useful spirit, month/day, movement, and real-world facts point in the
  same direction
- **中**: a main direction exists but manifestation or timing remains mixed
- **弱**: question is broad, useful-spirit selection is ambiguous, chart factors
  conflict, or critical real-world information is missing

Never use percentages.

## Reality Check

Before the final conclusion, ask:

- What facts are already known?
- Does the symbolic conclusion contradict explicit behavior, records, medical
  evidence, legal obligations, market constraints, or safety conditions?
- Is the suggested action reversible and proportionate?

Reality facts override contrary symbolic speculation.

## Writing Standard

For each conclusion:

1. state the judgment and its resolution
2. name the chart facts and classical rule
3. state the inference and contrary evidence
4. label speculation and confidence
5. give a restrained practical action

Avoid emotional reassurance, mystical filler, certainty, mind-reading, and
claims that the universe guarantees an outcome.
