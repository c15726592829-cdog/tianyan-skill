# 《周易·说卦传》 Source Records

### ZYSG-XIANG-001
- Status: A
- Book: 周易·说卦传
- Location: 说卦传第五章，八卦方位与运行次序
- Locator: Chinese Text Project, Book of Changes, Shuo Gua; received text also appears in public Zhouyi transcriptions
- Original: 万物出乎震，震，东方也；齐乎巽，巽，东南也；离也者，明也，万物皆相见，南方之卦也；坤也者，地也，万物皆致养焉，故曰致役乎坤；兑，正秋也，万物之所说也，故曰说言乎兑；战乎乾，乾，西北之卦也；坎者，水也，正北方之卦也；艮，东北之卦也，万物之所成终而所成始也。
- Operational rule: Eight-trigram direction images may corroborate a location or orientation after the person, object, or matter anchor has already been fixed.
- Conditions: Use only as one evidence layer after the question type, perspective, and primary anchor are selected.
- Exceptions: Do not infer a named modern city, exact route, or exact object from trigram direction alone.
- Conflicts: If Najia branch direction, Shi/Ying placement, or known geography contradicts the trigram image, disclose the conflict and lower resolution.
- Modern extension: Supports broad directions such as east, southeast, south, southwest or earth-place, west, northwest, north, northeast; modern place names remain speculative.

### ZYSG-BODY-001
- Status: A
- Book: 周易·说卦传
- Location: 说卦传第十一章，近取诸身
- Locator: Chinese Text Project, Book of Changes, Shuo Gua; public Zhouyi transcriptions under 说卦传
- Original: 乾为首，坤为腹，震为足，巽为股，坎为耳，离为目，艮为手，兑为口。
- Operational rule: Body correspondences can support object, health-symbol, or body-part imagery, but only as symbolic categories.
- Conditions: Use when the question asks about a held object, lost object, body part, symptom imagery, or visible image class.
- Exceptions: A body correspondence does not prove a modern product by itself.
- Conflicts: Modern exact objects require another independent evidence group beyond this trigram-body image.
- Modern extension: 坎为耳 can support ear-related objects such as earphones only when a second independent signal or fixed candidate set exists.

### ZYSG-NATURE-001
- Status: A
- Book: 周易·说卦传
- Location: 说卦传第十一章，远取诸物
- Locator: Chinese Text Project, Book of Changes, Shuo Gua; public Zhouyi transcriptions under 说卦传
- Original: 乾为天，坤为地，震为雷，巽为木，坎为水，离为火，艮为山，兑为泽。
- Operational rule: Canonical trigram natural images may corroborate environment, material, movement style, or broad matter type.
- Conditions: Use after Najia and useful-spirit evidence has selected the relevant line or trigram.
- Exceptions: Do not convert a natural image into a specific city, person, brand, or date.
- Conflicts: When a natural image conflicts with month/day strength or selected line evidence, treat it as secondary imagery.
- Modern extension: Natural images can describe setting classes such as water-place, fire/light, mountain/stop, marsh/open area, wood/wind, thunder/movement, earth/flat land, sky/high place.

### ZYSG-FAMILY-001
- Status: A
- Book: 周易·说卦传
- Location: 说卦传第十章，八卦父母男女象
- Locator: Chinese Text Project, Book of Changes, Shuo Gua; public Zhouyi transcriptions under 说卦传
- Original: 乾，天也，故称乎父；坤，地也，故称乎母；震，一索而得男，故谓之长男；巽，一索而得女，故谓之长女；坎，再索而得男，故谓之中男；离，再索而得女，故谓之中女；艮，三索而得男，故谓之少男；兑，三索而得女，故谓之少女。
- Operational rule: Trigram family images are symbolic positions and must not override the question's named person or Liuyao six-relative anchor.
- Conditions: Use as secondary imagery for family role, age-rank, or internal/external placement only after the primary anchor is fixed.
- Exceptions: Do not infer a person's gender, spouse identity, boyfriend, girlfriend, child, or companion solely from a trigram family image.
- Conflicts: If a named person or question grammar contradicts trigram family imagery, the named person and perspective govern.
- Modern extension: Useful for cautious labels such as elder/younger, initiating/responding, or family-position flavor, not exact identity.

### ZYSG-TEXT-001
- Status: A
- Book: 周易·说卦传
- Location: 说卦传第一章，作易与阴阳刚柔
- Locator: Chinese Text Project, Book of Changes, Shuo Gua
- Original: 昔者圣人之作易也，将以顺性命之理。是以立天之道曰阴与阳，立地之道曰柔与刚，立人之道曰仁与义。
- Operational rule: Received Zhouyi and Shuo Gua material supplies foundational image and textual meaning, not the complete later Najia operational apparatus.
- Conditions: Cite this when separating hexagram-image explanation from Liuyao useful-spirit and month/day judgment.
- Exceptions: Do not attribute later six-relative, hidden-spirit, or detailed timing procedures to the received Zhouyi text.
- Conflicts: Later operational manuals may govern Liuyao procedure, but their claims must be labeled as later manual rules.
- Modern extension: Output should separate original text translation from question-specific Najia analysis.
